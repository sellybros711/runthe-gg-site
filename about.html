About RunThe.gg

RunThe.gg is home to quick, free, browser-based sports games you can play the moment you land — no sign-ups, no downloads, no fuss. Just open the page and play.

Our first game is RunThePitch — World Cup Edition, a soccer game built for fans who want to jump in and have fun in seconds.

We're brothers who love sports and love building games. RunThe.gg is our project to make simple games and share them with anyone who wants to play. More games are on the way.
