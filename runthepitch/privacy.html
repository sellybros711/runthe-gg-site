Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 6, 2026

RunThe.gg respects your privacy. This page explains, in plain terms, what information is and isn't collected when you use our site.

Information we collect

Our games do not require an account or login, and they do not ask for or save personal information about you. You can play without signing up or giving us any details.

Cookies and advertising

We use third-party advertising, including Google AdSense, to help keep RunThe.gg free. Advertising partners, including Google, may use cookies to show ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. You can learn about and opt out of personalized advertising through Google Ads Settings and aboutads.info.

Analytics

We may use analytics tools, such as Google Analytics, to understand general, anonymous patterns like how many people visit and which games are popular. This helps us improve the site. It is not used to identify you personally.

Children

RunThe.gg is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date above will reflect any changes.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at hello@runthe.gg.
